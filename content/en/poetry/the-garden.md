Taking my own damn time, seizing a crown somewhere near the finish line. 

Story myth analogous to this deserving protagonist. Attracted to a caged wall, this castle came fast. 

Towers are tall but eventually they all fall to the ocean of time. A pattern born a matter mourned for a world scattered and torn. 

LET ME IN. Let me in to the garden, I'm a special sort of bargain. 

How do I seize it all without falling down. Crispy fire spurned. Surrendered agreements sworn, I've decided to be the matcher and the mad hatter with an open direction for all the blood splattered. 

How can I be a king thrown apart my ring.
