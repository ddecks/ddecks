Living on the leap year day, working every which way, doing all the things that I say.  

Staying precious without pressure, there may be somebody better, but gosh darn my heart's not going to falter. 

Do you make exceptions? Cause I got one question. Pressing hard with aggression, it may just be a suggestion. 

Spread love to fight the infection. Yes Love, I'm no debtor, would you be willing to send this letter, out to the cosmos, we got an answer. 

Our world is a blue dancer. Stay travelling, we will all change, but every molecule gets rearranged, shouting from long range, it seems like the pixels are bright. 

Lights are sworn to follow the speed that's born until they bend. Gravity ain't attaching.
