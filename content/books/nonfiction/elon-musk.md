Quick sum:   Some good  things about this book is that it shed some light on how plucky Elon actually is, he just continues to persist in the direction he knows is important for humanity to go to. He is unapologetic about demanding people's lives to get to that place. This is something that I think the human culture underappreciates in our current society. The drive absolute conviction to not only maintain momentum but accelerate it in a demanding way. I think many people rest on their laurels but Elon was an example of how that wouldn't be the case for him. I saw some controversy about Elon in regards to his video game scandal. It's alarming and there is certainly drama that surrounds this issue. 
Still my work ethic has never been higher and I think creating something that brings humanity to a direction that I would prefer is the an important path forward for me.
