Quick sum: Great book, honestly one of my favorite webnovels that I've started or read recently. Sunny is one of my favorite characters.
Still an incredibly satisfying book. I've gone back a reread this one I like it so much
Putting it in abandoned for now with the intention of going back to it a reading it again without a doubt in my mind. Right now Sunny and Nephis just killed Lord or Valor and (Queen of Worms? ) They became sovereigns and are among the most powerful that someone can be in humanity, not really sure what direction that the story can go from this point moving forward. I like how the powers scaled so reasonably and that Sunny could both be so powerful and yet face real danger at so many different points and time. Sunny now has like his own org thing going on and I'm not sure how I feel about that tbh. Rain still seems really cool as a char and she has lots of potential to develop I'm not just not sure where the story can go from here.
