Quick sum: 
Enjoying it so far, on Vol 4 as of writing this. It sort of bends a lot of the molds of xianxian work but honestly keeps the parts that I like, while also having an air of mystery to it as well. Jin is the MC and he has a dope rooster named Big D. It scratches all the itches that I feel I need scratched. Characters start to bleed quickly but I think that partly comes from the fact that I listen to the book while doing other things and don't completely devote myself to reading it on a daily basis, I have found that I haven't been properly syncing this stuff so I think I'll adopt a different method of synching and maybe start using S3 instead to manually publish and poll, we will see I'll give this a shot a couple more times and then maybe consider giving up on it. 
	Some ideas that I think are important from this book. Simplicity, generally finding joy in the simple things yields returns without expecting returns or even having a defined path. Community, community is really important so much more can be done by giving to others and letting them give back without having any consertnation. Sometimes hard choices have to be made between yourself and your own motivations for self sacrifice as a leader, this is a hard one and can only really come from trust in yourself and others, getting called out for your bullshit is only really possible from people that you love and care about. Being willing to learn and taking things in stride as they come. Give to the land and the land gives back is one of the enduring cultivating feelings of this book, everyone sees the main character's example and is awed by his willingness to do things not for himself but because he thinks it's the right thing to do in general. There is an underlying tone of flipping the world on it's head and instead of doing things because they seem right to the cultural world or society around you, doing things because they are a part of your internal guiding principal. Always treating others fairly no matter the power between you and them. Instead of taking advantage in short term opportunities remembering that every action has results that will build upon one another. Being curious, remembering that all people are different, coming into the world with different backgrounds, perspectives and being willing to see each path or dao as something internally meaningful.
