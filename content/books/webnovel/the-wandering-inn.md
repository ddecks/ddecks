# The wandering inn
Found an interesting [review](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ty65PoWv0I8) might start watching them more often
https://wanderinginn.com/
Pretty spectacular so far. I love Erin, I love the anitium, I love Pices, I love the goblins, I love the world and how it jumps between characters, the writing is smooth and great to listen to. 
Bummed the Horns of Hammerod got kidnapped by the mages waiting to see what happens with the brawl and erin afterwards, I think this may be her first time fighting mages.  Read the part about the brawl which was done pretty well, still haven't made much progress here though, not quite sure why, I think I will probably lay off this one for a bit and circle back at a another time. I think the main issue is that all these really long ongoing fantasys are a bit much to continue to keep attention for and then lots of things happen but if I'm listening at a point where I'm not focusing I miss out and don't feel as invested in the story. I also think scaling back my attention may be useful.
