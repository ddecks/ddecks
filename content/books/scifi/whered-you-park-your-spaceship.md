Quick sum:
Reading this now and it's about to finish, Gosh it was a pretty good one.
Broke almost all the scifi rules that one could imagine but the world itself didn't break. I was pretty well related to the MC and his goals were often aligned with me, he was the person who had things happen to him he was the observer that sees the world but he doesn't see how he fits into that world. 
Circling back after finishing, it was a great book, I recommended it to Chip so we will see if he ever followed up. Really delightful take on the best way to live a life and ask the question, who are we really working for?
