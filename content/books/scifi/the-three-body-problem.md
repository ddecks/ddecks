[Three-body Problem First Book](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Three-Body_Problem_(novel)) is the first book and most memorable title. In a [series](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Remembrance_of_Earth%27s_Past) of 3 books. 
Quick Thoughts:
pretty interesting, aliens, end of the universe, everything ended up resetting basically(ending review) . Technology(sophons, light_speed travel, hibernation, curvature propulsion),  scifi future was interesting(screens everyone, living underground, migrations of people) 

It also had human concepts and universal concepts that I found fascinating. 
	- Humans tending on the path of selfishness (wallfacer/wallbreaker project)
	 - the choice between love and mutual destruction (girl mc doesn't have deterrence or 
	conviction to escalate conflicts)
	-  Dark forest concept and deterrence theory (Luo Ji guy MC came up with it prolonging eventual human destruction),
	- the folding of dimensions, breaking down of dimensions (allusion to painting a picture) Different aliens and their viewpoints about life.
	- The eventual return of all matter back into the universe. 
	- Time relative to the speed_of_light
	- That there are not really any good or bad sides. Just different perspectives. And everyone is just trying to survive.
	- Less free will and more fate. (compared to western novels)
The most memorable part is the fairy tale that was in the final book it also serves as a cool way to describe basically all the incredible tech that was waiting to be adopted. Quick summary: kingdom with no stories, 2 princes 1 princess, king wise, secondary county has advanced tech, prince gets stuck on an island surrounded by giant fish that eat everything(impossible to pass), evil prince captures everyone in a painting using painter from secondary country if he sees ya you are done, umbrella protects you from being viewed by painter and becoming painting, princess goes to trapped prince he can only be viewed relatively ( this concept is sort of weird basically no matter how close or far he is he appears the same size to others)  she is able to travel to the island by using special soap from secondary country (mentions bubbles) everyone can see when you reach the speed of light though. Princess doesn't stay in the kingdom after the good prince defeats the evil one.
